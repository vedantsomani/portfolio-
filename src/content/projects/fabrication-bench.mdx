## What this mission proves

Fabrication work becomes more convincing when the page shows the distance between design intent and physical fit. The best evidence is the measured iteration: what changed, why it changed, and what the part looked like before and after.

## Evidence model

This mission should eventually include CAD thumbnails, slicer settings, print photos, caliper measurements, failed-print notes, and assembly shots. The current interface leaves exact slots for those assets.

## Current limit

The layout is ready, but the mission needs real media exports to become production proof rather than a structured placeholder.
