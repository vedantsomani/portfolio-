## What broke

Multi-hour prints paused mid-run on phantom filament-runout warnings. Each restart left a seam at the pause layer, which is a defect on visible surfaces.

## Why it broke

The runout sensor accumulated debris in the filament path and started reporting intermittent false positives. The workflow trusted the sensor without ever inspecting it.

## What changed

The sensor path is now part of the pre-print routine for long jobs, and the failed prints stay on the bench as evidence of why that check exists.
